About Us Page

Welcome to the Beach Guide App, your go-to source for real-time beach updates and alerts! We're passionate about making beach visits safer, easier, and more enjoyable. Whether you're checking the weather, surf conditions, or looking for a new spot to explore, our app helps you stay informed and make the most of your day by the water. This project was developed with the goal of connecting people to nature while keeping them informed about changing conditions. We're continuously working to improve your beach experience through accurate and accessible info.
